WHO AM I?

My name is Erik, a 21-year-old composer and sound designer based in Stockholm, Sweden. I am specializing in game music and dedicated to crafting innovative audio experiences that enhance the game experience. With a strong foundation in composition and sound design, I strive to push the boundaries of interactive audio in the gaming industry.

My work focuses on creating memorable musical themes and immersive soundscapes that elevate the player's experience and contribute to the narrative and emotional depth of games. I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy working within a team to achieve our collective goals.
